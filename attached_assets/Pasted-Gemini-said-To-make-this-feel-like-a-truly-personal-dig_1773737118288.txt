To make this feel like a truly personal digital gift, we’ll add a "Memories" Bento Box. This frame will have a glowing "polaroid" style, a soft holographic border, and a caption.

1. The "Photo of Us" Bento Component
You can place this code right under your CombinedHeader. It uses a placeholder image—you just need to replace the src with a link to your favorite photo together.

TypeScript

      {/* The Image Container */}
      <div className="absolute inset-0 p-4">
        <div className="relative h-full w-full rounded-2xl overflow-hidden border-4 border-white shadow-inner">

2. How to add your own photo:
To use your actual photo in the app:

Upload the photo to a service like Google Photos, Imgur, or even just drop it into your Replit public folder.

Change the URL: Find the src="..." line in the code above and replace the link with your photo's link.

If using Replit folder: If the photo is in your folder as us.jpg, change it to src="/us.jpg".

3. Where to put it in the Layout:
If you are using a Bento Grid (2 or 3 columns), place the PhotoBentoBox in the first column next to your Lead Generation tools.

TypeScript
<div className="grid grid-cols-1 md:grid-cols-3 gap-6 mt-8">
  {/* Column 1: The Photo Frame */}
  <PhotoBentoBox />

  {/* Column 2 & 3: Your Lead Gen or AI Tools */}
  <div className="md:col-span-2 bg-[#1e1f20]/40 rounded-3xl p-6 border border-white/5">
     {/* Your existing tools here */}
  </div>
</div>
The Final Touch
Since we are using Tailwind CSS, the hover:shadow-pink-500/50 will make the entire box glow pink when Shifa moves her mouse over your photo.
